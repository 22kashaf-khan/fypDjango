Automating the process of Garbage Collection

Objectives

January 1, 2023 by Kashaf Khan

By developing a garbage classification system using Computer Vision, Machine Learning algorithms, and IoT sensors, we plan to automate the process of garbage collection. The streets of our cities will be free from pollution by eliminating the heaps of garbage. The user will benefit from our reward point system in exchange for the trash they dump in our bin. Also, by continuously monitoring the level of garbage, the authorities can maintain a proper plan and schedule to pick up the litter before it overflows the bin. We picked this project because we believe that the hygiene problem of our city is a pressing concern. Using automated technology, we have devised a plan to give people a lifestyle with benefits and the authorities a better and proper systematic solution.

  • Provide instant classification at the initial stage (when the user disposes of trash in a bin).
  • Encourage proper waste management in the general public through Automated recycling
  • Reduce the pressure of garbage treatment resulting in harmless incineration.
  • Decrease the power generation of garbage and solve the problem of landfills.
  • Provide a solution to monitor and properly handle garbage for authorities
  • Provide an implementable solution to solve street littering problems in our cities
  • To increase the value of recyclable material and waste market for sustainable use.
